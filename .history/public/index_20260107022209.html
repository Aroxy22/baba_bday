üíå A Letter for You

Dear Baba,

Today is your birthday, and I wanted to take a moment to tell you how much you mean to me and how important your presence is in my life. This is just a small way of expressing what I feel and what I have learned from you over the years.

What I admire most about you is the way you always try to stay happy, no matter what life brings your way. You face every situation with calmness, strength, and a positive spirit. Watching you handle challenges with grace has taught me that true strength lies in attitude and perseverance. You inspire me to stay hopeful, work hard, and never give up, even when things feel difficult.

Your witty humour and your joyous, infectious laugh have always filled our home with warmth. You have a natural way of making everyone around you feel comfortable and happy. Beyond the laughter, your kind and loving nature toward our family stands out the most. You care for us selflessly and deeply, always putting our needs before your own. Through your actions, you have taught me the true meaning of love, responsibility, and sacrifice.

You are truly an all rounder in every sense. From cricket to singing, from playing the guitar to excelling at anything you choose to do, you show me that there are no limits when curiosity and passion guide you. You inspire me to explore my interests fearlessly and to believe that learning never stops. So much of what I know today, not just skills but values and perspectives, comes from watching you and learning from you.

Thank you for being my greatest inspiration, my guide, and my strongest support. I have learned so much from you, not just about life and work, but about being a good human being. I am incredibly proud to be your son and grateful for everything you have done for us.

Happy Birthday, Baba. May this year bring you good health, peace, and endless happiness.

With all my love,
